STS ensures that all federal and state clearances required to work within a school district are obtained. Requirements vary from state to state, although most states require a criminal background screening (Some require upfront fees) and the ability to provide official verification of the minimum education requirements for that state or school district, whichever may apply. Candidates with a deep desire to teach at the K-12 level and those who have a background working with youth in other capacities are well suited to work as a substitute teacher.
