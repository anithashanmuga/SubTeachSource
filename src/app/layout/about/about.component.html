About Us

Sub Teacher Source (STS) is a National Educational Staffing Agency connecting high quality substitute teachers to public, private, and charter schools in need. Our state of the art absence management system makes it easier than ever for schools and substitute teachers to connect for order fulfillment, manage time sheets and substitute payment, and successfully complete compliance requirements reporting.

With over 40 years of staffing experience, we have placed thousands and thousands of people. We approach each opportunity that we have with tenacity and a mission to fill each and every order possible. Our internal staff that includes certified teachers and former administrators who know what it feels like to be short-staffed and without many options. Our company motto is “Together Exceeding Expectations”. We make this a part of how we approach our daily duties and our long term strategic projects. We keep our company motto in mind as we work with our client partners as well as our substitute teachers.
